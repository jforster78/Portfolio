Who is JF Web

JF Web is about helping its clients achieve their online goals. I am passionate about creating new projects and relish new challenges. JF Web provides bespoke design and development to suit all. JF Web also offers its clients an alternative solution, where they can self-manage their own content; by using a content management service (CMS), such as the WordPress.

JF Web can also help with Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) to get you started online.

My Process

Information Gathering

A solid understanding of your goals, will determine how the web can be utilised to achieve those goals.

Planning

The planning of your website will involve a sitemap and which technologies to be implemented.

Design and Develpoment

Determining the look of your website will be through a wireframe and/or prototype. From that wireframe and/or prototype, I will create the functional website.

Testing and Delivery

A full testing of all links, functionality and scripts will be carried out before delivery.

Maintenance

The job does not stop there, if you require updating of information, changes and additions, these can be agreed. If you are intending to update your own content, a CMS maybe preferred.
