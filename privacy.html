1. What data do JF Web collect?

JF Web collects the following data:

  1. Personal identification information (Full name, email address, phone number, company and a brief description.)
  2. Google Analytics

2. How do JF Web collect your data?

You directly provide JF Web with most of the data collected. JF Web collect data and process data when you:

  1. Fill out and submit the form on JF Web’s website.

JF Web may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  1. Google Analytics.

3. How will JF Web use your data?

JF Web collects your data so that I can:

  1. Contact you about the Service you are enquiring about.

4. How do JF Web store your data?

JF Web securely stores your data within Google Firebase Real-Time Database.

JF Web will keep your contact details for 12 months. Once this time period has expired, I will delete your data by erasing it from the Google Firebase Real-Time Database.

5. What are your data protection rights?

JF Web would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights.

Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to request JF Web for copies of your personal data. JF Web may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification - You have the right to request that JF Web correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request JF Web to complete information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure - You have the right to request that JF Web erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that JF Web restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to JF Web’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability - You have the right to request that JF Web transfer the data that we have collected to another organisation, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, JF Web has one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact JF Web at: jfweb@outlook.com

6. What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit JF Web’s websites, JF Web may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

7. How do JF Web use cookies?

JF Web uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on my website, including:

  1. Google Analytics - to understand how you use JF Web website.

8. What types of cookies do JF Web use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, JF Web’s website uses:

  1. Google Analytics - JF Web uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to JF Web’s website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. JF web does not share this information.

9. How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

10. Privacy policies of other websites

The JF Web website contains links to other websites. JF Web’s privacy policy applies only to its website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
